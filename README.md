## 🌌 Vibes Check (About)

**zen.** is basically my cozy digital corner and an absolute appreciation space for **Jennie Kim** (金珍妮 💙). Built with a super clean **Apple Liquid Glass UI** concept, this site serves pure transparency, smooth animations, and dynamic video backgrounds. 

> "Find me in another world." 🪐 整个网站就是一个大写的 **高级感** (pure luxury vibes).

---

## 🎨 The Flex (Features)

* **🧊 Liquid Glass UI** — 氛围感拉满 (The atmosphere is unmatched). High-end glassmorphism effect that looks super crisp and premium.
* **🎥 Aesthetic Video BG** — Dynamic cinematic video playing in the background because static layouts are *so* last year.
* **🎵 On-Repeat Spotify Playlist** — 插入了我超爱的私人歌单 (Integrated my absolute fave tracks) to accompany you while you browse. Perfect for healing, chilling, or just vibing.
* **📸 Jennie’s Visual Room** — A curated, interactive photo gallery of Jennie Kim with smooth hover effects. Every image is optimized with alt text for that SEO grind.
* **🚀 Link Preview 100% Ready** — Fully optimized with Open Graph meta tags. When you share the link on chat apps, the preview card looks *so* professional, it’s insane.
* **📉 Scroll Reveal** — 丝滑滚动动画 (Silky smooth scroll animations) that glide into view as you scroll down.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

Keeping it light, fast, and completely vanilla. No heavy frameworks needed, 拒绝卡顿 (say no to lag):

* **HTML5** – Semantic structure + SEO meta data juice.
* **CSS3** – Custom variables, glassmorphism (`backdrop-filter`), Flexbox, Grid, and smooth transitions.
* **Vanilla JS** – `IntersectionObserver` logic for that lightweight scroll reveal effect.

## 🚀 Deployment (How to Host)

This site is 100% static, so deploying it takes like 2 seconds, 极其省心 (super hassle-free):

1. **GitHub Pages:**
   * Go to your repo **Settings**.
   * Hit the **Pages** tab on the left.
   * Under *Build and deployment*, set the source to **Deploy from a branch**.
   * Select `main` (or `master`) branch, hit **Save**, and boom—you're live! 🎉
